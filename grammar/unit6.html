# 6. 과거진행 ( I was doing )

  • - was / were + ...ing
  • - I was doing something
  • - I was doing vs I did
  • - 단순과거 + 과거진행
  • - 진행형 사용 불가 동사 ( know / want 등 )

# - was / were + ...ing

살펴보기

Yesterday Daewon and Joonghyuk played tennis. They began at 10:00 and finished at 11:30.
So, at 10:30 they were playing tennis.

They were playing ( = 그들은 테니스를 하고 있는 중이며 아직 끝나지 않았다. )

주어 과거동사 -ing
I/he/she/it
we/you/they		 was
were		 playing
doing
working 등

# - I was doing something

나는 ...하고 있는 중이었다. 동작이나 상황은 그 이전에 이미 시작되어 아직 끝나지 않고 진행 중 이다.

과거 과거에 하는 중 과거 현재
I started doing I was doing I finished doing now

예문

  • This time last year I was living in Bali.
  • What were you doing at 10:00 last night?
  • I waved to My friend, but he wasn't looking.

# - I was doing vs I did

과거진행 ( 진행 중인 동작 )

  • I was walking home when I met my friend.
  • Daewon was watching television when we arrived.

단순과거 ( 완료된 동작 )

  • I walked home after the party last night
  • Daewon watched television a lot when he was sick last year.

# - 단순과거 + 과거진행

종종 어떤 일을 하던 중 다른 어떤 일이 일어났을때 사용한다.

예문

  • Dongsuk burned his hand while he was cooking dinner.
  • It was raining when I got up.
  • I saw you in the park yesterday. You were sitting on the grass and reading a book.
  • I hurt my back while I was working in the garden.

그러나 차례로 일어난 일에 대해 말할 때는 단순과거를 사용한다.

예문

  • I was walking downtown when I saw Daewon. So I stopped, and we talked for a while.

비교

동석이가 도착하기 전 이미 저녁식사를 하고 있었다. 동석이가 도착한 후 저녁식사를 했다.
When dongsuk arrived, we were having dinner. When Dongsuk arrived, we had dinner.

# - 진행형 사용 불가 동사 ( know / want 등 )

예문

  • We were good friends. We know each other well. ( We were knowing )
  • I was having a good time at the party, but Jonghak wanted to go home. ( was wanting )

더 자세한 것은 4. 현재진행으로 잘 쓰지 않는 동사들 참조.
