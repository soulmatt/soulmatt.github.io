# 3. 현재진행과 단순현재 1 ( I am doing / I do )

  • - 현재진행 ( I am doing )
  • - 단순현재 ( I do )
  • - I always do / I'm always doing

# - 현재진행 ( I am doing )

현재진행은 말하고 있는 시점 이나 그즈음에 일어나고 있는 일 에 대해 말할 때 사용한다.
그 일은 아직 끝나지 않았다.

과거 현재 미래
I am doing

예제

  • The water is boiling. Can you turn it off?
  • Listen to those people. What language are they speaking?
  • Let's go out. It isn't raining now.
  • A: I'm busy.
    B: What are you doing?
  • I'm getting hungry. Let's eat.
  • My mother wants to work in Italy. So she is learning Italian.
  • The population of the world is increasing very fast.

일시적인 상황에는 현재진행을 사용한다.

예제

  • I'm living with some friends until I find a place of my own.
  • A: You're working hard today.
    B: Yes, I have a lot to do.

더 자세한 것은 Unit 1 참조

# - 단순현재 ( I do )

단순현재는 일반적인 일 또는 반복적으로 일어나는 일 에 대해 말할 때 사용한다.

과거-현재-미래
I do

예제

  • Water boils at 100 degrees Celsius.
  • Excuse me, do you speak Korean?
  • It doesn't rain very much in summer.
  • What do you usually do after work?
  • I always get hungry in the afternoon
  • Most people learn swim when they are children.
  • Every day the population increases about 200,000 people.

지속적인 상황 에는 단순현재를 사용한다.

예제

  • My parents live in Vancouver. They have lived there all they lives.
  • John isn't lazy. He works hard most of the time.

더 자세한 것은 2. 단순현재 참조.

# - I always do / I'm always doing

I always do ( something ) = 나는 늘/매번 ( something )을 한다.

예제

  • I always drive to work. ( I'm always driving )

I'm always doing something 은 의미가 다르다.

예제

  • I've lost my key again. I'm always losing things.
    ( = I lose things very often, perhaps too often, or more often than normal )
  • You're always watching television. You shoud do something more active.
  • Sangsoo is never satisfied. He's always complaining.
