# 105. 최상급( the longest / the most enjoyable )

  • - the longest / the most enjoyable
  • - 최상급 형태 ( -est / most ... )
  • - the + 최상급
  • - oldest / eldest
  • - ...에서 가장 ...한
  • - 최상급 + 현재완료

# - the longest / the most enjoyable

the longest / the most enjoyable 은 최상급 형태이다.

예문

  • What is the longest river in the world?
  • What was the most enjoyable vacation you've ever taken?

# - 최상급 형태 ( -est / most ... )

일반적으로 짧은 단어 + -est, most + 긴 단어 형태로 사용한다.
규칙은 비교급과 같다.
102. 비교급 ( cheaper, more expensive 등 )

변화

  • long -> longest
    hot -> hottest
    easy -> easiest
    hard -> hardest
  • most famous / most boring / most difficult / most expensive

일부 형용사들은 불규칙한 최상급 형태를 갖는다.

불규칙한 최상급 형태

  • good -> best / bad -> worst / far -> farthest / furthest

# - the + 최상급

일반적으로 최상급 앞에는 the를 쓴다.

예문

  • Yesterday was the hottest day of the year.
  • The film was really boring. It was the most boring film I've ever seen.
  • She is a really nice person, one of the nicest people I know.
  • Why does he always come to see me at the worst possible time?

최상급 vs 비교급

  • This hotel is the cheapest in town. ( 최상급 )
    This hotel is cheaper that all others in towm. ( 비교급 )
  • He's the most patient person I've ever met. ( 최상급 )
    He's much more patient than I am. ( 비교급 )

# - oldest / eldest

old의 최상급은 oldest 이다.

예문

  • That church is the oldest building in the city. ( the eldest building )

가족에 대해 말할 때는 eldest / oldest 둘 다 사용.

예문

  • My eldest son is 13 years old. ( 또는 My oldest son )
  • Are you the eldest in your family? ( 또는 the oldest )

# - ...에서 가장 ...한

최상급 + in + 장소 ( town, building 등 )

  • What is the longest river in the world? ( of the world )
  • We had a nice room. It was the one of the best in the hotel. ( of the hotel )

최상급 + in + 조직, 집단 ( class, company 등 )

  • Who is the youngest student in the class? ( of the class )

기간에 대해 말할 때는 일반적으로 of 사용

  • What was the happiest day of your life?
  • Yesterday was the hottest of the year.

# - 최상급 + 현재완료

최상급 뒤에는 흔히 현재완료 ( I have done ) 가 쓰인다. 7. 현재완료 참조

예문

  • What's the most important decision you've ever had to make?
  • That was the best vacation I've taken for a long time.
